Academic Programs

A comprehensive and structured curriculum designed for modern early childhood excellence.

Age: 1.5 - 2.5 Years

Playgroup Program

Our Playgroup program focuses on smooth transition from home to school. We emphasize sensory exploration, social interaction, and motor skill development through joyful activities.

  • Sensory-based learning
  • Social & emotional bonding
  • Introduction to basic vocabulary
Age: 2.5 - 3.5 Years

Nursery Program

The Nursery program introduces structured learning in a playful environment. We focus on language development, pre-math skills, and creative expression through music and arts.

  • Phonics & language foundation
  • Numbers & logical reasoning
  • Creative arts & performance
Age: 3.5 - 4.5 Years

Lower KG (LKG)

Our LKG curriculum is designed to build academic confidence. Children begin reading, writing, and understanding complex social concepts through collaborative projects.

  • Early literacy & writing skills
  • Environmental studies
  • Structured sports & fitness
Age: 4.5 - 5.5 Years

Upper KG (UKG)

The final year of preschool prepares children for the transition to primary school. We focus on advanced literacy, comprehensive math, and leadership qualities.

  • Advanced reading & comprehension
  • Primary school readiness modules
  • Public speaking & presentation

Building strong academic foundations and creativity in young learners since 2015.
